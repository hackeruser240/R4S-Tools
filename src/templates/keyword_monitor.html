Keyword Monitor: Keep an eye on and analyze your keyword rankings

The Rank 4 Sure Keyword Monitor helps you track your keywords’ performance and see how visible they are on search results pages. Whether you are managing SEO campaigns or updating your website, this tool shows your keyword rankings and, if you are not in the top spot, it also shows which competitors are ranking higher.

How to use this tool:

  • Enter one or more keywords separated by commas (e.g., SEO Masterz, website optimization).
  • Enable tokenization to allow partial matching of keywords across search results.
  • Click Scan Keywords to perform a Google Custom Search for each keyword.
  • The tool will analyze the top 10 search results and identify the first match for each keyword.
  • Results will be saved in a timestamped folder and displayed below, including matched titles and URLs.

This tool helps you monitor keyword visibility and ranking trends using Google’s Custom Search API.
